Our Core Values

  • Each citizen has the right and responsibility to participate in the decision-making process of the government.

  • Each citizen has the right and responsibility to participate in the development of his/her local community.

  • It is the obligation of the government to ensure that there is equal opportunity for citizen participation in all areas of government and community life.

  • It is the obligation of the citizen to monitor and address the government's actions in all areas of government and community life.

  • The provision of services and opportunities to individuals living and working in Peel should be equitable regardless of race, gender, creed, religious belief, ability, or sexual orientation.

What We Do

Community-Based Research

The SPCP's research program is designed to support community-based planning and citizen participation in Peel. It collects and analyzes data on Peels population, social problems and social services, as well as information on relevant social policies, best practices, social change models, etc. (Production of Information to Address Social Issues and Improve the Quality of Life for Individuals and Groups)

Public Education

The purpose of the Council's Public Education Program is to provide citizens and community-based networks and groups with information about Peels population, social issues and problems in Peel as well as information about how to address those issues. The Councils Research Program provides the information used in the Public Education Program. (Dissemination of Information)

Community Devlopment

This program provides a range of services to community-based groups, committees, task forces, etc. to enable them to initiate, plan, and evaluate their programs/events, etc. These services include organizational development, facilitation, training, research, mentoring, service planning, service coordination, and events planning. Two recent additions to this program are a) capacity-building for emerging non-profits and b) fiscal sponsorship of community groups and emerging non-profit agencies. (Application of Information)

Capacity Building

SPC-Peel works with small non-profits, emerging non-profits and citizen-based groups to help them develop their organizational capacity and carry out their community projects.
